This is MAINTAINIT TECHNOLOGIES L.L.C-FZ’s policy for Payments (“Policy”). This policy applies to activities called Services availed and engagement on its website/portal maintainit.ae and/or its related mobile applications (“Site”) and does not apply to Company activities that are "offline" or unrelated to the website. Any products or services that a user requests on our Site are referred to as “Service(s)”.

Modes of Payment

The Site accepts payment by cash, bank transfers, POS machine, payment link or through online debit/credit cards for its products and services. Services booked via the Site are also governed by the terms and conditions of respective merchant service providers paying for the services.

  • PAYMENT DEDUCTION

    • Users are charged when they book the service or when our service partner/provider has confirmed that the service has been delivered. The Company may or may not seek confirmation from the user that the service has been delivered before processing a payment.

  • MULTI-CURRENCY PRICED TRANSACTION.

    • The displayed price and currency selected by you, will be the same price and currency charged to the Card and printed on the Transaction Receipt. Where VAT or optional extras apply, the transaction amount will be shown to the user as “Total to pay” in AED.

  • PAYMENT CONFIRMATION

    • The Site user will receive payment confirmation by email on the email address given at the time of order, through a notification sent in an app, or both. The confirmation will be sent after the payment has been processed as soon as practically possible.

  • REFUND POLICY

    • In the event that the Company erroneously charges a user for a service that was not delivered, as confirmed by our service partner's delivery confirmation, the Company shall promptly reimburse the user the full amount. It is incumbent upon the User to promptly notify the Company of such occurrences by contacting customersupport@maintainit.ae within 24 hours of the scheduled service's due date. Any additional refunds, including those delineated above, shall be issued either as credits to the user's registered account on Maintainit Technologies or returned to the original mode of payment, at the discretion of the Company.

  • ADMINISTRATIVE CHARGES

    • Users who schedule services via the Site possess the capability to manage said services through either the website or mobile applications. Modification of service dates, times, or specific requirements is feasible, contingent upon execution at least 2 hours in advance of the scheduled service commencement. Failure to furnish adequate notice when canceling or altering services may incur administrative fees chargeable to the user. These fees are subject to the regulations stipulated within the Cancellations Policy and may undergo modification periodically. By availing themselves of the services offered on the Site, users acknowledge their consent to adhere to these policies and accept responsibility for any applicable charges. The Company retains the prerogative to employ any legal recourse at its disposal for the recovery of such charges or to restrict further utilization of the Site's services by the user, as deemed necessary.

  • Cancellations Policy

    • AED Administrative charges of AED 100 will apply in the following three scenarios;

    • Late Cancellations & Changes

      • For late cancellations or change requests (late is defined as less than 2 hours before the start of the service);

    • No Shows

      • Where a customer was not present to avail of the scheduled service and as a consequence the service was not delivered;

    • No Access to Property

      • In case the customer fails to arrange a gate pass/ access to the building for our team,
